
Business Problem

Background

AdventureWorks is a multinational retail company that sells products across multiple regions, customers, and product categories. As the business continues to grow, 
data is generated from various sources including sales transactions, customer records, product catalogs, returns, and regional operations.

The existing data is stored across multiple files, making it difficult for business users to generate consistent reports, monitor performance, and make informed decisions.

Problem Statement

Management requires a centralized Data Warehouse solution that integrates data from multiple business functions into a single source of truth. 
The current reporting process is time-consuming and lacks standardized metrics for sales, customer behavior, product performance, and regional analysis.

Without a structured analytical system, decision-makers face challenges in:

* Tracking sales performance across different years and regions.
* Identifying top-performing products and customer segments.
* Monitoring product return trends.
* Measuring business growth over time.
* Creating consistent reports for stakeholders.

 Proposed Solution

Develop an Enterprise Retail Data Warehouse using SQL Server to consolidate data from multiple source files into a structured dimensional model.

The solution will include:

* Data extraction from raw source files.
* Data cleaning and transformation.
* Staging layer implementation.
* Fact and dimension table design.
* Star schema modeling.
* Business reporting views.
* Interactive Power BI dashboards.
* Advanced analytical reporting using SQL and DAX.

Expected Business Value

The proposed Data Warehouse will enable business users to:

* Access a centralized and reliable source of data.
* Monitor key performance indicators in real time.
* Improve reporting efficiency and accuracy.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify revenue growth opportunities.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

Key Business Questions

1. What are the overall sales trends across different years?
2. Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
3. Which customer segments contribute the most sales?
4. Which territories perform best and worst?
5. What is the product return rate by category?
6. How does customer purchasing behavior change over time?
7. Which products require strategic attention due to declining performance?
8. What insights can help improve overall business performance?

Project Scope

This project focuses on building an end-to-end Retail Data Warehouse and Analytics Platform using AdventureWorks data. 
The project covers data ingestion, transformation, warehouse modeling, SQL analytics, and Power BI reporting to support business decision-making.
